Our Placement Process

Our placement process ensures that students are well-prepared for the job market. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Registration

Students must register on the placement portal to start receiving job opportunities.

Step 2: Resume Submission

Students upload their updated resumes to apply for job openings.

Step 3: Pre-Placement Talks

Companies host pre-placement talks to introduce students to their work culture and job roles.

Step 4: Interview

Students participate in interviews, both technical and HR, with hiring companies.

Step 5: Offer and Placement

Selected students receive job offers and are placed in their respective companies.
